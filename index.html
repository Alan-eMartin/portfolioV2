About

As a former Chef having worked at some of Canada’s top restaurants, I bring a unique set of skills to Front End Web Development. With my attention to detail, eye for design and my ability to work with others, I can turn problems into solutions. After 10 years of working in the culinary industry, I decided to leave my passion for food at home and to use what once drove me as a Chef in Web Development. As a recent grad of Juno College’s Web Development Immersive Bootcamp, I have learned what it takes to make a website responsive and accessible using modern frameworks like React JS, while also building strong foundations in JavaScript, HTML5, CSS and SCSS.

The ability to continuously learn and a build upon existing knowledge in Web Development was a big reason why I decided to switch careers. My passion for learning is what made my career as a chef a successful one and I’m excited to do the same as a Jr. Front End Web Developer.
